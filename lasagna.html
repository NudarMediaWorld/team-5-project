2 servings

1 hour

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup beef broth, 1 tbspoon tomato paste
  • 1 tspoon dried basil and oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese, 1 egg beaten
  • 4 cups shredded Mozarella and 1 1/2 cup grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1/2 tspoon salt, 1/4 tspoon black pepper
  • 8 - 10 lasagna noddles
  • Spinach, Mushrooms, Bell pepper, sausage, Bacon (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 375oF (190oC), Cook ground beef until browned.
  2. Add onion, garlic, marinara sauce, beef broth, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt and pepper. Simmer 30 minutes
  3. Combine ricotta cheese, Mozarella, Parmesan cheese, egg, salt and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Cook Lasgna noodles. spread meat sauce in the bottom of a 9 x 13 inch baking dish, layer noodles, ricotta mixture and cheese. Repeate for 3-4 layers
  5. Top with mozarella and Parmesan cheese, cover with foil; bake 30 minutes. remove foil; bake 10-15 mins or until golden brown
  6. Serve Hot!!

Lasgna Pasta

2 servings

1 hour

Lasagna is a classic italian dish made with layers of pasta, sauce, cheese, and various filings. its origins date back to ancient Greece and Rome, but mordern version emerged in naples Italy in the 18th century

The word Lasagna comes from the Greek work Laganon meaning flat bread, Lasagna has been a staple in Italian Cuisine for centuries

NOTES

Use high quality cheese and sauce, Don's overcook noodles, experiment with fillings, let Lasgna rest for better flavor
