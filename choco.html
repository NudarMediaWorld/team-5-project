2 servings

1 hour

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 1/ 4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 1/ 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 tbspoons baking powder, 1 tspoon salt
  • 1/ 2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup whole milk, at room temperature
  • 2 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1 tspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 1/ 2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tbspoon milk or heavy cream

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 350oF (180oC). Greese two 9-inch (23cm) round pan, whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a seperate bowl, whisk cocoa powder and milk, add eggs one at a time, whisking well, add vanilla extract, combine dry and wet ingredients; mix until smooth and pour batter into pan, bake for 30-35 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean
  3. Beat Butter until creamy, gradually add confectioners' Sugar, cocoa powder and milk, beat until smooth and creamy
  4. Let cakes cool completely place one cake layer on a serving plate, spread frosting on top. Add second cake layer Frost the outside
  5. Serve Hot !!.

Chocolate Cake

2 servings

1 hour

Chocolate cake is a rich, decadent dessert originating from ancient Mesoamerica, where cacao beans were first used. The modern version emerged in europe in the 17th century.

Today, it's a beloved treat worldwide. Chocolate cake has antioxidant properties due to cocoa's flavonoids

NOTES

Use high-quality cocoa powder, dont overmix batter, adjust frosting consistency experiment with flavors (e.g; nuts, coffee)
