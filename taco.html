2 servings

50 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 pound ground beef or any alternative
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced
  • 1/2 medium bell pepper, diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning (or homemade blend)
  • 8-10 corn tortillas
  • Shredded cheese (cheddar or Monterey Jack)
  • tomatoes, shredded lettuce, diced avocado
  • sour cream, Salsa, Cilantro

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Brown ground beef in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion, bell pepper and jalapeno; cook until softened
  2. Add garlic and taco seasoning; cook 1 minute, simmer 5-10 minutes, stirring occassionally.
  3. Warm tortillas by wrapping in a damp paper towel and microwaving 20-30 seconds
  4. Spoon meat mixture onto a torilla. Add desired toppings
  5. Fold totilla in half to enclose
  6. Serve Hot !!.

Tacos

2 servings

50 minutes

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a small, foldede corn tortill filled with various ingredients, such as meats, cheeses, vegetables, and sauces.

The word "taco" originated from the Aztecs and means "half" or "in the middle". Tacos originated in Mexico over 2,000 years ago.

NOTES

Use fresh, high-quality ingredients, experiment with different meats and toppings, adjust seasoning to taste, warm tortillas for better flexibility
