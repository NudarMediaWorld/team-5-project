2 servings

50 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 pound thinly sliced beef
  • 2 tbspoons olive oil
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 tspoon paprika
  • 1 tspoon cumin
  • 1/2 cup tahini paste
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2teaspoon Coriander
  • Tomatoes, onions, cucumbers, parsley
  • 4-6 pita breads, 1/4 cup water

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a bowl whisk together olive oil, garlic, paprika, cumin, coriander, salt and pepper.
  2. Add sliced meat, marinate for 30 minutes to 2 hours. Grill or cook meat in a skillet until browned and cooked through.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together tahini paste, lemon juice, water, garlic, salt and pepper. Adjust consistency and taste.
  4. Warm pita breads. Slice cooked meat into thin strips.
  5. Add meat to pita bread. Top with tomatoes, onions, cucumbers and parsley.
  6. Drizzle tahini sauce and wrap. Serve Hot with desired drink.

Shawarma

2 servings

50 minutes

Shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern Street food originating from Turkey and the Levant region. it's a juicy, flavorful sandwich made with thinly sliced meat, served in a warm pita bread with vegetables and tahini sauce.

Shawarma means "turning" in Turkish, refering to the meat's rotation on a vertical splits if making chicken Shawarma add yogurt and cumin to marinade
