2 servings

50 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 granulated sugar
  • 2 tspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 tspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup usalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • 1 3/4 cup heavy cream or buttermilk
  • 1 egg beaten
  • 2 cups sliced strawberries and honey
  • 4 tbspoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tspoon vanilla extract

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 375oF (190oC). In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder and salt
  2. Add cold butter, use a Pastry blender or fingers to work into the mixture until crumbly. Pour in cream or buttermilk stir until dough forms.
  3. Turn dough onto floured surface; gently knead 2-3 times, roll out to 1-inch thickness, cut into round or any shape, bake for 18-20 minutes. Allow to cool
  4. In a bowl mix sliced strawberries with granulated sugar, let sit for 15-30 minutes, allowing juices to release
  5. In a bowl, whip heavy cream until soft peaks form. Add granulated sugar and vanilla extract, continue whipping till stiff peaks form.
  6. Split shortcake into halves horizontally. spoon sweetened strawberries over the bottom half top with whipped cream and place the top half and fresh strawberry(if desired)

Strawberry-Shortcake

2 servings

50 minutes

Strawberry-Shortcake is a classic American dessert consisting of sweet biscuits (shortcake), fresh strawberries and whipped cream. This beloved treat originated in the United States in the 19th century and remains a popular dessert during strawberry season

You Can add blueberries or raspberries to the strawberries, and use different types of milk or cream. Substitute honey with Maple syrup if honey is unavailable, Top with chopped nuts or chocolate shaving if required

NOTES

Use fresh strawberries for the best flavor, dont overmix the shortcake dough, adjust sugar to taste, serve immediately or store components for up to 24 hours
