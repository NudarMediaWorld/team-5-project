2 servings

50 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 tspoon salt and 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced & 1/2 tspoon salt,
  • 1/4 tspoon black pepper & 1/4 tspoon italian seasoning
  • 2 tbspoons olive oil, 1 onion chopped
  • 2 cups canned crushed tomatoes, 1 cup beef broth, 1 tbspoon tomato paste
  • 1 tbspoon dried basil and oregano, salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pound spaghetti

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat oven 400oF (200oC)
  2. combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, egg, Parmesan cheese, parsley, garlic, salt, pepper, and Italian Seasoning; mix well with hands or spoon. form into 1 1/2-inch (3.8 cm) balls. Bake 18-20 mins or until cooked through.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan. Add onion and garlic; cook until softened, Add crushed tomatoes, beef broth, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt & pepper
  4. Simmer 30 minutes, stirring occassionally, boil salted water; cook spaghetti 8-10 mins, reserve one cup pasta water, drain spaghetti
  5. Add cooked meatballs to sauce; simmer 5 mins, add reserved pasta water to sauce stir.
  6. Serve meatballs and sauce over spaghetti.

Spaghetti & Meatball

2 servings

50 minutes

Spaghetti and meatballs is a classic Italian-American dish that originated in the united states in the early 20th century. it's a hearty, comforting meal consisting of spaghetti pasta meatballs made from ground meat, and a tangy tomato-based sauce

The dish was originated in the United States, and meatballs are called "Polpette" in Italian.
