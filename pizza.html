2 servings

3 hours

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups warm water
  • 2 tspoons sugar
  • 1 tbspoon active dry yeast
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tspoon salt
  • 2 tbspoons olive oil
  • 2 cups crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup mozarella cheese
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced, pepper
  • 1 tspoon dried basil and oregano
  • Mushroom

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Combine warm water, sugar and yeast, let sit for 5-10 mins, Add flour, salt and olive oil; mix until dough forms
  2. Knead 5-10 mins; place in greased bowl, cover and rise 1-2 hourse, preheat oven to 425oF (220oC), Punch down dough; roll out 1/4-inch thickess
  3. Combine crushed tomatoes, olive oil, garlic, oregano, basil, salt and pepper.
  4. Spread sauce over dough, leaving 1/2-inch border. Add toppigs (Mushroom).
  5. Sprinkle Mozarella and Parmesan cheese. Bake 15-20 mins or until crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  6. Serve Hot with any drink to complement it.
